Highest Motorable Road

Highest Motorable Road Tour Package

  • 4 Nights / 5 Days
  • Ladakh

PLACES COVERED: Leh Palace-Tsemo Castle- Shanti Stupa- Sham valley- Hall of Fame- Magneic hill-Sangam-Khardungla top-Shey Palace-Thiksey Monastery- Hemis Monastery- Rancho School-Pangong Lake- Changla pass.
Itinerary:

Day 1 : Arrival in Leh 3500 meters / 11560 feet & Local Sightseeing

Arrival at Leh, half day rest for acclimatizationand post lunch visit Sham Valley including Indian Army Hall of Fame-A war museum dedicated to the brave martyrs of Indian Army, Magnetic Hills, Confluence of Zanskar& Indus river andGurudwara Pather Sahib ( 60 Km). O/N stay at Leh.

Day 2 : Srinagar – Sonmarg – Srinagar 168 Kms.

Full day local sightseeing to Indus valley including Shey Palace- the former Palace of the Namgyal king of Ladakh, Thiksey monastery-an impressive complex rising tier upon tier on a hill above the village, resembling Potala Palace of Tibet and Hemis monastery which is one of the richest and largest monastery of Ladakh andhave a huge collection of Artifacts, Paintings etc at their museum and enroute visit Druk whiteLotus school of 3 idiots fame (85 km) O/N stay at Leh

Day 3 : Day trip to Pangong Lake (5-6 hour one way)

After early Breakfast leave for Pangong Lake (14,500 ft) through Changla Pass 17,350 ft, which is the third highest motorable road in the world. Pangong, is the highest salt water Lake in the World, shared by two countries India & China. Upon arrival, visit the location of 3 Idiots also known as shooting point. Later on you can take a walk along the scenic turquoise colored Lake and enjoy its beauty and after spending sometime return to Leh. (160 km one way). O/N stay at Leh.

Day 4 : Khardungla pass & local sightseeing

After breakfast drive to Khardungla- world’s highest motorable road at 18,830 ft above sea level, enjoy cup of soup/coffee at World’s Highest Motorable pass, enjoy the picturesque view of snowcapped mountain and enroute visit Leh palace- Palace of the erstwhile Royal King of Ladakh, Tsemo castle and Shanti Stupa which offers you a panoramic view of entire Leh town, Indus valley and lofty mountain of Zansker & Ladakh range(100 km). Evening Free for leisure and shopping at bustling Leh market (100 km). O/N stay at Leh.

Day 5 : Return from Leh

Return from Leh, your memorable tour ends.
