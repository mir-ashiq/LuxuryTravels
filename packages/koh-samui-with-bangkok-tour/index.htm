Koh Samui with Bangkok Tour

Koh Samui with Bangkok Tour

  • 4 Nights / 5 Days
  • Thailand
Day 1 : Bangkok to Koh Samui

After arriving at Bangkok airport, proceed to the domestic airport to board your flight to Koh Samui. Upon arrival, meet an IHPL representative who will help you with an assisted transfer to the hotel. Post-check-in formalities, the day is at your leisure.

Day 2 : Koh Samui

On day 2, have your breakfast and get all set for partaking in sightseeing of the mesmerizing Koh Samui Island. The first visit of the day is to the extraordinary rock formations of Hin Ta & Hin Yai, popularly known as Grandfather and Grandmother Rocks. Later, continue towards Na-Muang Waterfalls, two fresh water falls, placed close to each other. Indulge in fun activities here. Now move to Nothan, Koh Samui’s capital, to splurge in shopping. You can not only get your hands on some amazing stuff like home décor products, Thai fruits, eco-friendly beauty products, etc but can also taste some amazing lips-smacking authentic seafood delicacies. Bring your city tour to an end by visiting the very popular attraction and landmark of Big Buddha statue, a 12 m tall status made in gold in 1972. The evening is at leisure. Overnight stay.

Day 3 : Koh Samui-Bangkok

Have your breakfast. Later, get transferred to the airport to board your flight to Bangkok. An IHPL representative assists you with the check-in process at the hotel. The afternoon is scheduled for a temple and city tour of Bangkok. The last visit is to the Royal Palace or Grand Palace. Overnight stay in Bangkok.

Day 4 : Bangkok

The fourth day after breakfast is free. Spend the day as per your liking and partake in the activities of your choice. Explore the nearby attractions, interact with the locals, binge on lip-smacking local delicacies, or relax in your hotel—overnight stay in Bangkok.

Day 5 : Departure

On the last day, after breakfast, get transferred to Bangkok Airport to board the flight for your onward journey.
