Bali Holiday Tour

Bali Holiday Tour

  • 5 Nights / 6 Days
  • Bali
Day 1 : Arrive Bali

On arrival at Denpasar airport, you will be met by a local representative and get transferred to your hotel (check-in after 1400 hrs). The evening is at leisure. Overnight in Ubud.

Day 2 : Kintamani Valcano & Ubud tour

Breakfast at the hotel’s restaurant, start for an impressive experience to visit Celuk Village. This traditional village is famous for gold and silver handicrafts and Mas Village for its wood carving. Kintamani is famous for its view of an active volcano, the magnificent rice paddy terrace at Tegalalang, Ubud Bali’s cultural heartland. Overnight in Ubud.

Day 3 : Ubud to Kuta - Enroute Tanah Lot tour

Breakfast at the hotel’s restaurant, pick up from the hotel’s Lobby to Kuta. While in en-route we will continue to Tanah Lot Temple. This is the best part of this tour, as Tanah Lot is best known for its beauty, and you will get to see the Silhouette view of the temple. Overnight in Kuta.

Day 4 : Jatiluwih and Bedugul

Breakfast at the hotel’s restaurant, pick up to scenic Lake District at the mountain resort of Bedugul. Travel the backtracks and the non-touristy trail to watch the Balinese doing their routine chores. Next, Jatiluwih a nominated for designation as a UNESCO World Heritage site offers a beautiful view of contoured rice terraces irrigated using an impressive communal water system developed by Balinese farmers. Overnight in Kuta.

Day 5 : Full Day Water Sports - Uluwatu Temple

Breakfast at Hotel’s restaurant, we will pick you up from the hotel around 1100 hrs (11:00 am). After the pickup, we will continue to Benoa Harbour, for water sports. We have included 01 round each of banana boat ride, jet ski & Parasailing. After the water sports, we will continue to Uluwatu temple, built on a steep drop slope of more than 100 meters which is continually under seawater wild abrasion. It is famous for its view during the Sunset. Overnight in Kuta.

Day 6 : Check out & Departure

After breakfast at the hotel’s restaurant, depending on your flight timings, and hotel area, we will pick you up from the hotel, around 02 – 03 hours, before your flight departure time. Until your pick up time, your time is free for shopping and exploring the city on your own.
